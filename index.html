Wave

The modern day person who is bombarded with seemingly infinite amounts of information. We need cognitive downtime: time for our biological information processing system to analyze and file the data received during it's conscious state.

Our solution was to create an application that allowed users to reflect on things that happened throughout their day. Inspired by journals, we created an app that would sync with your calendar and photos. Wave provided a new experience for users, they could now easily take cognitive downtime breaks that they enjoyed.
